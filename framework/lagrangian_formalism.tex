\section{Lagrangian 形式}对于场,变分原理是：
\begin{equation*}
    \begin{aligned}
        0 &= \delta I \\
        &= \int d^4x \left[ \frac{\partial \mathscr{L}}{\partial \varphi ^{(a)}} \delta\varphi ^{(a)} + \frac{\partial \mathscr{L}}{\partial (\partial_\mu \varphi ^{(a)})} \delta(\partial_\mu \varphi ^{(a)}) \right] \\
        &= \int d^4x \left\{ \left[ \frac{\partial \mathscr{L}}{\partial \varphi ^{(a)}} - \partial_\mu \frac{\partial \mathscr{L}}{\partial (\partial_\mu \varphi ^{(a)})} \right] \delta\varphi ^{(a)} +
        \partial_\mu \left[ \frac{\partial \mathscr{L}}{\partial (\partial_\mu \varphi ^{(a)})} \delta\varphi ^{(a)} \right] \right\}
    \end{aligned}
\end{equation*}

\noindent
我们假设场在无穷远处是趋于$-\infty$,所以第二项自然的消失,我们得到了场的运动方程。
\begin{tcolorbox}
    \begin{equation}
    \frac{\partial \mathscr{L}}{\partial \varphi ^{(a)}} - \partial_\mu \frac{\partial \mathscr{L}}{\partial (\partial_\mu \varphi ^{(a)})} = 0
\end{equation}
\end{tcolorbox}
